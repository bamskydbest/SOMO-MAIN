WEB DEVELOPMENT


Web development, also known as website development, refers to the tasks associated with creating, building, and maintaining websites and web applications that run online on a browser. It may, however, also include web design, web programming, and database management. Web development is closely related to the job of designing the features and functionality of apps (web design). The term development is usually reserved for the actual construction of these things (that is to say, the programming of sites). The basic tools involved in web development are programming languages called HTML (Hypertext Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), and JavaScript.

PRODUCT DESIGN


Product design and user interface/user experience (UI/UX) design are two intertwined disciplines that focus on creating exceptional user experiences. While product design encompasses the overall physical and functional aspects of a product, UI/UX design specifically deals with the digital interfaces and interactions that users engage with. Product design considers the entire lifecycle of a product, from concept development to manufacturing and beyond. It involves designing tangible objects, considering factors such as aesthetics, ergonomics, materials, and manufacturing processes. The goal is to create products that are visually appealing, functional, and aligned with user needs.

UI/UX DESIGN


UI/UX design focuses on the digital interactions between users and a product or service. UI design concentrates on the visual elements of an interface, including layout, typography, colors, and icons, aiming to create a visually pleasing and intuitive digital environment. UX design, on the other hand, focuses on the overall user experience, encompassing factors such as usability, accessibility, and user satisfaction. When product design and UI/UX design come together, they create a seamless and delightful user experience. A well-designed product not only looks visually appealing but also provides intuitive and user-friendly digital interfaces.
